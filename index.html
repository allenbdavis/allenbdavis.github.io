Hello there!

Welcome to my website. My name is Allen Davis. I am a teacher at the International School of Boston in Cambridge, MA. I earned my Ph.D. in Astrophysics from Yale University in 2020, where I researched new techniques to discover exoplanets. Today, my goal is to give young people the opportunities and inspiration to explore our universe, improve our planet, and to become the best version of themselves.

My Background (CV)

I defended my PhD in Astrophysics from Yale University in 2020. I graduated from Williams College in 2014. I have coauthored 17 peer-reviewed papers about topics in astrophysics.

Teaching

I have taught physics and math at ISB since 2020. I was a TA for Yale undergraduate astronomy classes from 2015-2020.
